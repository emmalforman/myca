Unrelated business ideas and opportunities to revisit.

## Logistics Company for Small CPG
- Small/emerging CPG brands struggle with fulfillment, 3PL costs, pallet minimums, and freight
- Existing 3PLs are built for volume — small brands get bad rates, slow onboarding, poor service
- Opportunity: boutique logistics / fulfillment shop that specializes in emerging food & bev brands
- Could bundle: warehousing, pick & pack, DTC fulfillment, wholesale distribution, cold chain
- Potential synergy with Myca — many members are small CPG founders who'd be ideal customers
- Revenue: per-order fees, storage, value-added services (QC, kitting, relabeling)
